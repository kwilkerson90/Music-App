The King of Pop

The King of Pop, Michael Joseph Jackson, was born on August 29, 1958. He had a larger-than-life career and life, and he left an unquestionable mark on the culture and the world. MJ released 10 studio albums, 3 soundtrack albums, 1 live album, 39 compliation albums, 10 video albums, and 8 remix albums. Since his death, 2 albums were posthumously released.
